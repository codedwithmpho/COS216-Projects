Boeing 787 Dreamliner

Manufacturer

Boeing Commercial Airplanes

Description

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is a long-range, wide-body, twin-engine jet airliner. It is the first airliner with an airframe primarily made of composite materials. The 787 is designed to be 20% more fuel efficient than the Boeing 767 it is intended to replace.

Seats

290 passengers (typical 3-class configuration)
